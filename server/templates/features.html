Core Features

Everything you need in one app

Daily verses, audio Bible, smart search, and a full memorization toolkit — all wrapped in a warm, distraction-free design.

🌤

Daily Verse

Start each day with a fresh verse from Scripture, plus a daily wisdom proverb and a structured reading plan following the Book of Common Prayer.

🔊

Audio Bible

Listen to any verse read aloud from the ESV. Tap the speaker icon on any verse card to hear the passage — perfect for commutes, walks, or quiet moments.

🔍

Smart Search

Look up any Bible reference instantly, or search by keyword to find verses across the entire English Standard Version.

🔖

Themed Collections

Browse 18 curated collections — Hope, Love, Strength, Peace, Forgiveness, and more. Each theme gathers the most powerful verses on that topic.

🏆

Bible Challenge

Test your Bible knowledge with Jeopardy-style trivia. Choose from 23 categories, pick your difficulty, and earn points with streak bonuses.

🔥

Streaks & XP

Build a daily habit with streak tracking and XP points. Every verse you read, practice, or review earns progress toward your goals.

Memorization Toolkit

6 ways to make Scripture stick

From flashcards to AI-generated songs, every mode is designed to move verses from short-term to long-term memory.

🃏

Flashcard

Classic flip cards — see the reference, recall the verse, flip to check.

✋

Fill in the Blank

40% of words are hidden — tap to reveal them as you recall each one.

🔀

Scramble

Words are shuffled — drag them back into the correct order.

❓

AI Quiz

AI-generated questions test comprehension, context, and recall.

✍️

Type It

Type the verse from memory with real-time accuracy feedback.

🎵

Sing It

AI turns any verse into a song — choose Gospel, Hymn, Pop, R&B, and more.
